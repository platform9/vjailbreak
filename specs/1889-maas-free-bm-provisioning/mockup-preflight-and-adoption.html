High-impact adoption improvements
1
Guided setup wizard phase 1
First-visit wizard: connect vCenter → see clusters → click a host → follow 3-step drain/maintain/convert flow. Removes "where do I start?" confusion.
2
Cluster conversion ETA phase 1
Show "~4h to convert full cluster" based on average migration time per VM × remaining VM count. Helps operators plan maintenance windows.
3
Batch host operations phase 2
Checkbox-select multiple empty+maintenance hosts → "Convert All (3) to PCD". Speeds up final phase of large cluster migrations.
4
No-MAAS "quick start" badge phase 2
Prominently show "Works without MAAS" when IPMI provider is selected. Key adoption message for customers blocked on MAAS installation.
5
Rollback / re-add to VMware hint phase 1
On conversion failure, show "Exit Maintenance and re-add to VMware cluster" as recovery action. Reduces fear of irreversibility.
